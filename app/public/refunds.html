Refund Policy.

EFFECTIVE 12 JULY 2026 · CINEFOLIO.DEV
The short version: try the studio free before you pay a cent. If you pay and we fail to deliver your Director's Cut, you get every cent back — no forms, no fight. If we deliver and it misses your brief, tell the director within 14 days and we make it right or refund you.

01Try before you buy

Every account includes free AI films and unlimited use of The Set. That is deliberate: you should know exactly what the studio produces before spending money. Most refund policies exist to manage disappointment; ours starts by preventing it.

02If we fail to deliver

If your paid Director's Cut never reaches a premiere-ready state — the pipeline fails, the production stalls past reasonable effort, or we simply cannot produce what was promised — you receive a full refund, promptly and unconditionally. Undelivered work is never kept money.

03After delivery — the 14-day window

Every Director's Cut includes revision messages to the director; that is always the first tool, and it fixes most things. If your delivered cut materially misses the brief and revisions have not fixed it, request a refund within 14 days of delivery at info@cinefolio.dev with your order reference. We review in good faith — we have no interest in keeping money for work you don't love. Refunds after the 14-day window, or where the delivered work matches the brief, are at the studio's discretion.

04EU withdrawal right

The Director's Cut is digital content produced and delivered on demand. By placing the order you expressly request immediate performance and acknowledge that, under EU consumer rules (Directive 2011/83/EU), the 14-day right of withdrawal lapses once delivery of the digital content has begun with your consent. This does not affect sections 02–03 above — our own promises are stronger than the statutory minimum — and it never limits mandatory consumer rights in your country.

05How refunds are paid

Purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy (Sold through Link, LLC) as merchant of record. Approved refunds are issued by Lemon Squeezy to your original payment method, typically arriving within 5–10 business days depending on your bank. Taxes collected at checkout are refunded with the purchase.

06What refunds don't cover

  • Free-tier productions (nothing was paid)
  • Changed minds after the film was delivered as briefed and the withdrawal right has lapsed (section 04) — though talk to us anyway; the revision messages exist for exactly this
  • Hosting time already elapsed on a delivered film

07How to request

Email info@cinefolio.dev from your account email with the order reference (it's on your receipt and in your console's order history). We respond within 2 business days.
